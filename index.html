Overview

The PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana is an initiative by the Government of India to promote rooftop solar installations. It aims to provide up to 300 units of free electricity per month to 1 crore households.

Benefits

  • Subsidy of ₹30,000 for 1kW systems.
  • Subsidy of ₹60,000 for 2kW systems.
  • Subsidy of ₹78,000 for 3kW systems.
  • Reduction in electricity bills.
  • Contribution to a greener environment.

Eligibility

  • Must be an Indian citizen.
  • Own a house with a suitable rooftop.
  • Have a valid electricity connection.
  • Should not have availed any other solar subsidy.
